About Us

Company History

Founded in Milwaukee in 1963, J&R Industrial Sales Inc. has become one of the largest distributors of abrasive products for Wisconsin's foundry industry. Over the past 50 years J&R Industrial has grown its business across the United States. J&R also serves the welding, steel fabrication, ornamental iron , and construction industries.

Specializations

Through AbrasiveGuys.com you can view J&R Industrial's product offerings , which show a wide variety of abrasive products including: large resinoid snagging and hot pressed cutoff wheels, cup and cone type wheels, coated abrasives such as: sanding belts, resin fiber discs, cartridge rolls, and surface conditioning products for grinding, blending, and finishing. Most recently we have added two new lines: cutting tools, and industrial brushes.
